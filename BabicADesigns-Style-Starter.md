# BabicADesigns — Style Starter

A starting point for a Claude Design project. Paste this into a new claude.ai/design project as the brand brief, then refine as designs come back.

## Brand essence

Soulful systems, not hustle. Uskok heritage (Senj, Croatia) — quietly rebellious, never bowing to "empire" trends. Depth that cuts through hype. Baka (grandmother) wisdom as the emotional core. Warm, direct, strategic, never sterile.

## Color palette

Editorial warm-neutral, not glossy or corporate. Suggested starting hexes (refine against real assets once you have them):

| Role | Color | Hex (suggested) |
|---|---|---|
| Background / base | Cream | `#F5EFE4` |
| Secondary surface | Oat | `#E8DCC8` |
| Primary neutral | Taupe | `#A89684` |
| Accent / depth | Soft olive | `#7C7A5E` |
| Text / ink | Warm near-black | `#2B2520` |
| Highlight (sparingly) | Burnt terracotta | `#B5603F` |

Avoid: bright saturated tech-SaaS colors, neon, pure white/black, anything "glossy."

## Typography direction

- Serif or humanist display face for headlines — editorial, not corporate (e.g. something in the spirit of a modern serif like Fraunces, Lora, or similar warm serifs).
- Clean sans for body copy, generous line height — calm, readable, unhurried (e.g. similar to Inter, Söhne, or General Sans).
- Avoid condensed/bold tech sans faces — they read "hustle," which is explicitly against the brand.

## Visual style

- Editorial photography, not Instagram-glossy
- Natural light, candid not posed, shallow depth of field, 35mm film aesthetic
- Generous whitespace, slow pacing — no busy/urgent layouts
- Minimal UI chrome; let warm neutrals and type carry the design

## Voice guardrails (for UI copy, microcopy, CTAs)

- Warm, direct, quietly rebellious. Smart-friend tone, not guru tone.
- Short sentences; occasional longer one for rhythm.
- No urgency-driven CTAs ("Don't miss out," "Limited time"). Soft closes over hard sells.
- Words to use: soulful, strategic, honest, clarity, rhythm, alignment, depth.
- Words to avoid: hustle, grind, secret, hack, viral, explode, guru, blueprint, "game-changer," "10x," "unlock," "crushing it."
- Signature phrases (use sparingly): "Soulful systems." "Strategy with a soul." "Stay yourself."
